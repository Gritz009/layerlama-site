Process

From Concept to Print

Every project follows a refined workflow to deliver prints that exceed expectations.

01
💬
Consultation
We discuss your vision, requirements, and constraints. I recommend the best material and approach for your project.
02
✎
Design & Slice
Models are optimized for 3D printing — wall thickness, supports, infill, and orientation are tuned for quality.
03
⚙
Print & Finish
Prints are monitored for quality. Post-processing includes support removal, sanding, and optional painting or coating.
Services

What I Offer

From one-off prints to small production runs, Layer Lama has you covered.

📦
Custom Prints
Send your STL or 3MF file and choose your material, color, and finish. Fast turnaround on single prints or batches using our fleet of Bambu Lab and Creality printers.
🛠
Prototyping
Rapid iteration on product enclosures, mechanical parts, and custom fittings. PLA-CF and ASA available for engineering-grade strength. Design feedback included.
🎨
Art & Décor
Sculptural pieces, vases, lithophanes, and decorative objects. Unique gifts and home accents printed to order in a variety of colors and finishes.
💻
Design + Print
Don't have a 3D model? No problem. We can design print-ready models from sketches, photos, or descriptions — and bring them to life.
♔
Miniatures & Gaming
High-detail resin prints for tabletop gaming, D&D characters, terrain pieces, and collectible miniatures at 0.05mm layer resolution.
🎓
Workshops & Education
Introduction to the world of 3D printing. Hands-on demos, printer walkthroughs, and workshops for beginners, schools, and businesses looking to explore additive manufacturing.
